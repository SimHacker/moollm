## EvalEye vs Eval-X

| Candidate | Issue |
|-----------|-------|
| Eval-X | Too close to "X" platform, legally risky |
| EvalEye | Original, evocative, symbol-ready |

### Why Eval-X Is Dangerous

- "X" is a single-letter trademark actively defended
- "Eval-X" reads as "Evaluation of X" or "X-related tool"
- Parody/criticism safe harbor is weak for product names
- Brand confusion is the legal test, not intent

### Why EvalEye Works

- No trademark adjacency
- Conceptually independent
- Thematically stronger
- Ages well
