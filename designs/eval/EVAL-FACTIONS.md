# Eval Factions: Design Document

> *These are not jokes. They are procedural antagonists and ideological actors.*

---

## What Are Factions?

Factions are **collective meta-players** in the EVAL simulation:
- Decentralized swarms
- Bureaucratic institutions
- Belief systems that promise perfect judgment

They compete, collide, and co-opt one another, shaping the world through **evaluation rather than force**.

### ğŸ” QEval

*"Evaluate the evidence yourself."*

The ethical version of grassroots epistemology. What "do your own research" would look like if it meant what it said.

**The QAnon Critique:**

| QAnon Says | QAnon Does | QEval Does |
|------------|------------|------------|
| "Do your own research" | Follow the drops | Actually evaluate sources |
| "Question everything" | Never question Q | Question everything, including yourself |
| "Trust the plan" | Outsource judgment | Own your judgment |
| "Where we go one, we go all" | Conformity | Declare your bias, expect disagreement |
| "The truth is out there" | The truth is in-group | Truth is contested, earned, provisional |

**Core Principles:**

1. **Evaluate the evidence yourself** â€” but know your limits
2. **Declare your priors** â€” what you believed before looking
3. **Seek disconfirmation** â€” try to prove yourself wrong
4. **Show your work** â€” transparent reasoning, not secret knowledge
5. **Update publicly** â€” when wrong, say so loudly
6. **Distrust certainty** â€” especially your own

**The Key Difference:**

| QAnon | QEval |
|-------|-------|
| Secret knowledge for the elect | Open evaluation for everyone |
| Certainty as virtue | Uncertainty as honesty |
| Leaders decode for you | You decode, you own it |
| Enemies everywhere | Biases everywhere (including yours) |
| The Storm is coming | Judgment is ongoing |
| WWG1WGA | Where we evaluate, we diverge |

**Motto:** *"The research is never done. The evaluation is never final. The bias is always declared."*

**Procedural Rhetoric:**

QEval is what happens when you take "do your own research" seriously instead of as a tribal signal. It's uncomfortable because:
- You might be wrong
- You have to show your work
- You can't hide behind a leader
- Your enemies might have a point
- Certainty is a red flag, not a virtue

QAnon offers the *feeling* of independent thought with the *comfort* of groupthink. QEval offers actual independent thought with the *discomfort* of being responsible for your own conclusions.

## Why This Works

| Quality | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Power is explicit | No hidden control |
| Ideology is embodied | Not abstract |
| No faction is "good" | All have failure modes |
| Player choices matter | But outcomes aren't clean |

## Ethical Safety

This is NOT exploitation because:
- Power is explicit
- Assumptions are inspectable
- Outcomes are logged
- Ideology is embodied
- Harm is contextualized
- Agency is preserved

**The game doesn't moralize. It shows how factions function.**
