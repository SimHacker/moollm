## The Columbo Interrogation

### The Method

MOOLLM's adversarial-committee skill uses what we call "Columbo interrogation":

> "Oh, just one more thing..."

The technique:
1. Appear to accept the answer
2. Turn away as if satisfied
3. Pause
4. Turn back with "But there's one thing I don't understand..."
5. The question that unravels everything

### Applied to AI

When reviewing AI output for slop, sycophancy, or hidden ideology:

1. Accept the response initially
2. Then: "But wait, why did you say 'It's important to note'?"
3. The AI realizes it was slopping
4. Course correction
