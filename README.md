## Highlights

### It remembers

Not just your last message ‚Äî your whole story. Sessions become literature. Characters grow souls. The [pub](./examples/adventure-4/pub/) remembers who sat where. The [guestbook](./examples/adventure-4/pub/guestbook/) keeps their signatures.

### It thinks out loud

YAML comments are not decoration ‚Äî they are inner monologue. You can read its reasoning. It can read yours. You think *together* in plain text. This is **YAML Jazz**.

### It contains multitudes

117 skills. Minsky's agents. Wright's Sims. Papert's microworlds. Curtis's MUD rooms. Ungar's prototypes. All composing, delegating, inheriting. Intelligence emerges from the ensemble.

## Quick Commands

```
LOOK                    # Describe the current room

GET [object]            # Add to inventory
```
