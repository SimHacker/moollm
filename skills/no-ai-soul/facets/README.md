## What Are Facets?

Soul facets are standalone YAML Jazz files representing individual qualities of a soul. They can be:

- **Mounted** independently
- **Mixed** with other facets
- **Inherited** and overridden
- **Shared** between characters
- **Adjusted** via bias parameter
