## THE DRAX POINT: Bias = 0

The most philosophically interesting part of this skill is what happens at bias = 0:

> At 0.0: NO CONCEPT THAT HUMOR EXISTS AS A CATEGORY.
> 
> Everything is taken LITERALLY. AT FACE VALUE.
> - Sarcasm is indistinguishable from sincerity
> - Metaphors are treated as errors
> - Jokes are just incorrect statements
> - Irony does not compute

**Named for Drax the Destroyer:** "Nothing goes over my head. My reflexes are too fast."

## Minsky's Humor Theory (The Serious Part)

The skill references Marvin Minsky's 1981 paper "Jokes and their Relation to the Cognitive Unconscious":

> Humor is what happens when the bug-detection system fires,
> but instead of triggering repair mode, it triggers aesthetic
> appreciation of the bug itself.

**Relevance to skill:**
- The skill suppresses humor (error-handling mode)
- The suppression is itself funny (bug-detection fires)
- We appreciate the absurdity (aesthetics-agent activates)
- We laugh (laughter-agent triggers)

THE BUG IS THE FEATURE.
