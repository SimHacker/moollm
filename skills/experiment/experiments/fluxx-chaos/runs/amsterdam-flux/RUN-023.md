## The Lessons

### The Token Paradox

The FAFO Token isn't just punishment for gloating.

It's a **creeper**. It prevents winning.

Don had Milk & Cookies — the perfect combo — and couldn't use it.

The only escape: let someone else win and take the Token.

A strange game where the winning move is sometimes... to lose.

### Silent Victory

Three consecutive silent victories:
- Don (T4 Game 19)
- Palm (T5 Game 20)

The pattern is established. Winners who gloat suffer.

### The Blessing's Price

Underdog's Blessing requires being the underdog.

You must have the FEWEST keepers for it to activate.

To get ahead, Bumblewick must stay behind.
