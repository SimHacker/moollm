## Understanding The Game

### How Fluxx Works

Fluxx is a card game where the rules constantly change. You start with:
- **Draw 1, Play 1** â€” draw one card, play one card

But "New Rule" cards change everything:
- Draw 5? Play All? Hand Limit 0? 
- The rules shift constantly

### Winning

To win, you need to match the current **Goal**. Goals require specific combinations of Keepers:
- "Milk and Cookies" = Milk + Cookies
- "Rocket to the Moon" = Rocket + Moon
- "Peace and Bread" = Peace + Bread

But there's always only one Goal at a time â€” and anyone can change it.

### The Chaos

This is why Fluxx is chaos:
- You might be one card away from winning
- Someone plays a new Goal
- Now you need completely different Keepers
- And you just drew a Creeper

Welcome to Fluxx. Welcome to chaos.
