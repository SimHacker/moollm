## The Lesson

*The grotto is quiet. Post-storm, post-pardon, post-revelation.*

Don pours sharp cheddar cheese for the table. Even for Bumblewick, who won't drink it.

"Poker teaches you things."

Palm nods. "What did you learn tonight, little one?"

Bumblewick thinks. Actually thinks, instead of panicking.

"That the scary people aren't always winning. And that winning people can be quiet about it."

He looks at Donna.

"You trapped me like I trapped Palm."

"Yes."

"Is that what poker is? Trapping?"

Donna considers. "Poker is... knowing when to speak. And when to let others speak for you."

Palm's fur shimmers slightly. "That's the best definition I've heard in 122 years."

Don raises his glass.

"To mutual destruction. And mutual respect."

They drink. Even Bumblewick tries a sip.

*He makes a face. But he tries.*
