## The Core Philosophy

Traditional education: **absorb knowledge**.
Constructionism: **build knowledge**.

| Traditional | Constructionism |
|-------------|-----------------|
| Read about bicycles | Ride the bicycle |
| Study documentation | Build a skill |
| Memorize patterns | Create microworlds |
| Consume | Construct |

## Why Debugging Is Learning

Traditional education: mistakes are failures.
Constructionism: **mistakes are data**.

When your construction doesn't work:
1. You notice the gap between expectation and reality
2. You investigate the cause
3. You modify and retry
4. You've learned something concrete

Debugging IS the learning. Not a prerequisite.
