# Society of Mind (schemapedia: `society-of-mind`)

**Meta_model family.** **Marvin Minsky**’s architecture: simple agents, larger **societies of mind**, and emergent behavior—indexed for navigation, **not** as a validator or wire format.

## Why it matters here

MOOLLM uses SoM as **orientation** for how [k-lines](../k-lines/README.md), [minsky-frame](../minsky-frame/README.md), and other Minsky-flavored mechanisms fit together. The registry entry prevents mistaking “schema” for only JSON/XML.

## What’s distinctive

- **Delegate**: [`society-of-mind`](../../../../society-of-mind/SKILL.md) skill—book-level and protocol context.
- **Not operational interchange**: no XSD/JSON Schema role; use those mechanisms for payloads.
