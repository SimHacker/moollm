## Prompt

Aerial/bird's-eye view of a living, breathing maze structure. The maze is 
biological, organic, made of pulsing fungal tissue. Each chamber glows a 
different color corresponding to the food markers left within. Fractal 
patterns extend from each room, connecting through luminescent mycelium 
networks. The perspective shifts impossibly, Escher-like, as if the maze 
exists in multiple dimensions simultaneously.

cross-legged, surrounded by mushrooms, a single eye (The Thing That Watches) 
gazing up from beside them.
