# 🗿 Room G — The Carved Face

> *"You are in a maze of twisty little passages, all alike."*

This one has a **FACE** carved into the wall. An ancient, weathered face that might once have been a king, or a god, or possibly just someone's uncle who was really good at posing.

Its empty eyes seem to follow you as you move.

*"Don't mind me," you say to it. "Just passing through."*

The face does not respond. Obviously. It's a face carved in rock.

---

## ⚠️ DANGER: DARK

This room has **no lighting**. Enter without a lit lamp and you will be eaten by a grue.
