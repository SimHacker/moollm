# 🕸️ Room E — The Cobwebs

> *"You are in a maze of twisty little passages, all alike."*

This one is FULL of cobwebs. Ancient, dusty cobwebs that suggest the spiders who made them have long since moved on to more profitable real estate.

The webs catch your lamp light and sparkle like... well, like dusty spider webs.

You resist the urge to scream. Barely.

---

## ⚠️ DANGER: DARK

This room has **no lighting**. Enter without a lit lamp and you will be eaten by a grue.
