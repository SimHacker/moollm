# ✍️ Room C — The Scratch Marks

> *"You are in a maze of twisty little passages, all alike."*

The walls are covered with desperate scribblings of previous adventurers trying to map their way out. Most are just tally marks. One reads "HELP". Another reads "DROPPED MY SANDWICH HERE".

Someone has helpfully written "THIS WAY OUT →" with an arrow pointing at a solid wall.

---

## ⚠️ DANGER: DARK

This room has **no lighting**. Enter without a lit lamp and you will be eaten by a grue.
